Air Premia Livery has just came out!

AIR PREMIA LIVERY has just came out! Project76 B767 Livery 4K supported! REALISTIC, and HIGH QUALITY CABIN INCLUDED Air Premia is the first hybrid airline to start operating during the Corona Pandemic (2020). Of course, it belongs to the category of low-cost airlines, but it is good enough to compare services with regular airlines. The aircraft also uses the latest aircraft, the B787. Unlike other low-cost airlines, free in-flight meals, in-flight entertainment, and Wi-Fi are provided free of charge. The price is of course cheaper. In this way, we have made Air Premia, a new concept hybrid airline that is emerging these days, into B767. The original plane is a 787. Installation You need Project76 B767(freeware) to fly this livery! After download our livery unzip it, and move it to B767->liveries folder.
